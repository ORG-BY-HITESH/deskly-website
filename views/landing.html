How it works

Install once. It handles the rest.

1

Install & choose a vault

Pick any folder on your machine — Documents, a USB, your Dropbox. That's where your data lives. Human-readable JSON you can open in any editor.

2

It runs in the background

Every second, Deskly checks the foreground window using Windows UI Automation. No browser extension, no proxy, no kernel driver. Just reads the window title.

3

See exactly where time went

Dashboard shows today at a glance. Reports show week, month, year, all-time. Best day, worst day, weekday patterns, top apps, category breakdown — all yours.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

No bloat, no subscription, no upsell. A focused tool that does its job well.

Browser intelligence

Knows which site you're on across 56 browsers. Chrome, Brave, Arc, Tor, Zen — no extension, no proxy. Detects 88 websites automatically.

Every app, every second

Win32, UWP, PWAs. 1-second polling via UI Automation API. Sub-second accumulator means zero time lost on app switches.

App limits

Daily time caps with a soft overlay that follows the app. Three 5-minute extensions per day, then it's done. Respects your decision.

Deep reports

Week, month, year, all-time up to 5 years. Best and worst days, weekday patterns, top 15 apps, category breakdown with percentages.

Goals & streaks

Set a daily target. Build streaks. Unlock 10 achievements — First Step, Hat Trick, Weekly Warrior, all the way to Monthly Master.

Break reminders

Five rotating types: eye rest (20-20-20), deep breathing, movement, hydration, mental reset. Configurable 20–60 minute intervals.

Custom categories

Group apps and sites into Development, Social, Entertainment — or create your own. 12 colors, custom labels, AI-powered suggestions.

Export everything

Full JSON dump or structured CSV with columns: date, app, category, duration. Your data is never locked in. Ever.

System tray native

Runs silently in the background. Shows today's time at a glance. Pause tracking, quick resume, double-click to open. Starts with Windows.
